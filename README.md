# 📊 Exploratory Data Analysis of E-Commerce Sales Data

## 📌 Overview
This project analyzes an e-commerce sales dataset using Power BI to uncover trends in product performance, customer behavior, payment preferences, and revenue patterns. The project was completed as part of the DecodeLabs Data Analytics Internship Program.

## 🎯 Objectives
- Identify top-performing products and customers.
- Analyze customer payment preferences.
- Examine order status distribution.
- Evaluate revenue trends over time.
- Generate actionable business insights.

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Power BI
- DAX
- Data Visualization
- Data Analysis

## 📈 Key Metrics
- **Total Orders:** 1,200
- **Total Revenue:** ₦1,264,761.96
- **Average Order Value:** ₦1,053.97
- **Median Order Value:** ₦823.62

## 🔍 Key Insights
-  Chairs generated the highest revenue, followed by Printers and Laptops.
-  Online Payment was the most frequently used payment method.
-  High-value customers were identified through revenue contribution.
-  Cancelled and returned orders accounted for 41.4% of total orders.
-  Revenue peaked between May and June before declining in later months.

## 💡 Recommendations
-  Increase focus on high-performing products.
-  Implement customer retention strategies for top customers.
-  Investigate the causes of cancelled and returned orders.
-  Maintain secure and efficient online payment systems.
-  Replicate strategies that contributed to peak revenue periods.

## 🏁 Conclusion
The analysis provided valuable insights into sales performance, customer behavior, and operational challenges. The findings demonstrate how data-driven decision-making can support business growth and improve overall business performance.
